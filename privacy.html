Privacy Policy for Home OS

Last Updated: [Current Date]

Welcome to Home OS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our WhatsApp assistant.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use Home OS, we collect and store:

  • Account Data: Your WhatsApp phone number and WhatsApp profile name.
  • Conversation Data: The text, voice notes, and images you send to the bot, as well as the bot's replies.
  • Personal Information: Information you explicitly ask the bot to save (e.g., tasks, financial expenses, grocery items, journal notes, family size, timezone, and currency preferences).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve the Home OS service. This includes managing your household, setting reminders, tracking expenses, and analyzing your shopping patterns to provide intelligent suggestions.

3. Third-Party Services

To function, Home OS relies on third-party services. By using Home OS, you acknowledge that your data is shared with:

  • Meta (WhatsApp): For sending and receiving messages.
  • AI Providers (Groq, Google Gemini, OpenAI): Message content, voice notes, and images are sent to these AI models for processing and transcription. These providers process the data to generate responses but are not permitted to use your private data to train their public models.

4. Data Sharing & Households

Home OS is designed for households. If you invite family members or helpers to your "Household" using an invite code, they will be able to see shared data such as groceries, events, and shared tasks. Private notes and personal finances remain visible only to you unless explicitly shared.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • You can delete your entire account and wipe all your personal data (tasks, notes, expenses, settings) at any time by sending the command: /deleteme
  • Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data using industry-standard security practices. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information (like full credit card numbers or passwords) to the bot.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the bot implies acceptance of those changes.
